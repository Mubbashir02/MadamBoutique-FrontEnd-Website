About us

Madam's Boutique entered the realm of fashion in 2019 as a small design house.Despite working with minimal setup Madam's made a name for herself, gaining tremendous appreciation from clients. With this as inspiration along with own passion to create exquisite couture, the brand SanaAbbas ventured into a full-fledged fashion house.
From modest beginnings, Madam's Boutique is now a thriving business run by a dynamic duo, each one bringing their own expertise to the table.
Showcasing her collection in Europe, America, and the Middle East, Sana Abbas has made a benchmark in couture fashion. Her repertoires of creations have been adorned at shows with the likes of International Bridal Fashion Week (IBFW) Atlanta, Pakistan Fashion Extravaganza (PFE) London/Los Angeles, and The Perfect Wedding UAE. Madam's Boutique also showcased at Bridal Couture Week 2015 for the third consecutive year.

The House of Madam's Boutique is an insignia of style and sophistication. Its philosophy is to create elegant and chic bridals & couture wear. Meticulous handwork, intricate embroidery, coupled with impeccable finishing and attention to detail makes every outfit breathtaking. Continuously delivering unique designs year after year sets Madam's Boutique as one of the most sought after brand in the fashion industry.
